About Mysuru

Beautiful places in mysore

Mysore (or Mysuru), a city in India's southwestern Karnataka state, was the capital of the Kingdom of Mysore from 1399 to 1947. In its center is opulent Mysore Palace, seat of the former ruling Wodeyar dynasty. The palace blends Hindu, Islamic, Gothic and Rajput styles. Mysore is also home to the centuries-old Devaraja Market, filled with spices, silk and sandalwood.

Heritage and Palaces:

  • Mysore Palace: A stunning landmark and the former residence of the Wodeyar dynasty.
  • Heritage Structures: Mysuru boasts numerous historical buildings and monuments that reflect its royal past.
  • Cultural Capital: The city is often called the "Cultural Capital of Karnataka" due to its rich traditions in music, dance, art, and literature.
